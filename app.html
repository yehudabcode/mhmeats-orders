📖 One-Time Setup Guide  ▼ click to expand

Takes about 10 minutes. Do this once only.

STEP 1 — Create the Google Sheet

Go to sheets.google.com → New spreadsheet → name it MH Meats Orders.

In Row 1, type these column headers exactly:
Timestamp  |  Customer  |  CustomerName  |  Day  |  ProductKey  |  ProductName  |  Quantity  |  Status

STEP 2 — Add the Apps Script

In your Google Sheet: click Extensions → Apps Script. Delete all existing code. Open the file MH_MEATS_APPS_SCRIPT.gs (included in your download), copy all of it, paste it in. Click Save (💾).

STEP 3 — Deploy as Web App

Click Deploy → New Deployment → Web App.
Set: Execute as → Me  |  Who has access → Anyone.
Click Deploy → Authorise → copy the URL it gives you.

STEP 4 — Paste URL in two places

1. Paste it into the field above and click Save.
2. Open mhmeats_customer_portal.html in Notepad. Find YOUR_APPS_SCRIPT_URL_HERE and replace it with your URL. Save the file.

STEP 5 — Upload to Google Drive

Upload mhmeats_customer_portal.html to Google Drive. Right-click → Share → Anyone with the link → Viewer.
Copy the sharing link. The customer links in the table above will update automatically once you save your Apps Script URL.
